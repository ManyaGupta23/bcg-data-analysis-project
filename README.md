📌 Project Overview
Completed a real-world job simulation focused on advising a hypothetical client on multi-channel advertising campaign performance.
The project involved analyzing simulated marketing data to evaluate campaign effectiveness, identify performance gaps, and provide strategic recommendations aligned with business growth objectives.

📊 Key Responsibilities
Analyzed simulated customer conversion data across multiple marketing channels
Assessed campaign effectiveness across different audience segments
Identified key performance trends and optimization opportunities
Evaluated channel-wise ROI and engagement patterns
Developed structured, data-driven strategic recommendations

Data Analysis

Strategic Problem Solving
Recommendation Framing for Stakeholders

📈 Key Outcome
Delivered clear, structured, and actionable recommendations to improve campaign ROI, enhance channel efficiency, and support data-driven marketing decisions.
